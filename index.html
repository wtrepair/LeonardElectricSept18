Licensed contractor

The safe choice for every electrical job

Licensed Electrical Contractors are the only businesses in Ontario legally authorized to do electrical work in your home or facility. We handle notifications and keep your project aligned with the Ontario Electrical Safety Code.

  • Qualified to meet Ontario Electrical Safety Code requirements.
  • Workplace Safety and Insurance Board coverage.
  • Arrange ESA notifications for completed work.
  • Helpful guidance on choosing a licensed contractor.
