Service Call Terms — Leonard Electric

By checking “I agree,” you confirm you understand and accept the following terms for this service call:

This appointment is a paid service call. Leonard Electric is not attending to provide a free estimate or quote-only visit. The purpose of the visit is to diagnose and/or perform the requested work during the appointment, as conditions allow.

Rates and billing

  • The minimum charge is $250, which includes dispatch/show-up and up to the first hour on-site. If the work takes less than one hour, the charge remains $250.
  • After the first hour, labor is billed at $120 per hour.
  • After the first hour, time is billed in 30-minute increments (0.5 hours).

Materials and parts

Materials/parts are extra and are billed in addition to labor.

If the customer supplies materials/parts and they are incorrect, missing, incompatible, defective, or insufficient, the customer agrees that:

  • time spent troubleshooting delays is billable, and
  • time spent obtaining the correct materials (including travel time) is billable at the same labor rate and billed in the same 30-minute increments, plus the cost of any materials Leonard Electric purchases.

Leonard Electric does not provide warranty coverage for customer-supplied materials/parts and is not responsible for failures caused by them.

No estimate visit / unknown conditions

Because this is not an estimate visit, the customer understands that hidden or unforeseen conditions may exist (for example: aluminum wiring, concealed junctions, unsafe prior work, damage behind drywall, limited access, code issues, or other conditions not visible until work begins). These conditions may increase the time required, change the method of repair, and/or require additional materials. Any additional time required is billed at the rates above.

If a safety hazard is discovered, Leonard Electric may pause or stop work until it can be addressed. Time spent up to that point remains billable.

Access and safety

The customer is responsible for providing safe and timely access to the work area (including electrical panels, rooms, and equipment), and for securing pets and hazards. If Leonard Electric arrives and cannot access the work area, the $250 minimum charge still applies.

Payment authorization

By agreeing, the customer authorizes Leonard Electric to charge the $250 minimum for the service call and to invoice (and/or charge the payment method on file, if applicable) for any additional labor and materials in accordance with these terms.
